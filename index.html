VC Discord Mod

After downloading the Discord Mod folder, open Discord. Then, open the "zip "folder and right-click on the "setup.exe "file. Run it as an administrator. If it doesn 't work, close your Windows Security. If it works, Discord will either be restarted or closed. Open Discord again, and then click on "VCinstaller.exe."Once it opens, click on the green "Install "button. Discord will be restarted again. Now, check your Discord settings. Go to the bottom of your settings, where you will find a "Plugin "button and a "Theme "button. You can try adding as many plugins as you want. One of the best plugins is "fakenitro,"which allows you to screen share at higher video quality and gives you 60fps while sharing.
